Case 88 Evidence - mTLS Communication

mTLS handshake evidence:
- Service-to-service traffic uses mTLS (mutual TLS) with x509 client certificate validation.
- TLS handshake includes certificate chain verification and client certificate identity checks.
- Internal PKI issues and rotates certificates for service workloads.

Reject/no-cert evidence:
- Requests with no certificate are rejected.
- Invalid client cert is denied with handshake fail / bad certificate / unknown ca signal.
- Unauthorized service caller is forbidden when certificate required policy is enforced.
