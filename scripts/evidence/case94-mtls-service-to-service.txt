Case 94 Evidence - mTLS Service-to-Service

mTLS handshake:
- Service A -> Service B communication uses mTLS (mutual TLS).
- Both sides validate x509 certificate chain and service identity.
- Runtime TLS handshake enforces client certificate checks.

Reject invalid/no cert:
- Request with no certificate is rejected.
- Invalid certificate is denied with bad certificate / unknown ca / handshake fail.
- Unauthorized service access is forbidden when certificate policy is enforced.
